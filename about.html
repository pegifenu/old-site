About

Hello, I'm Pegifenu, a programmer and artist currently pursuing a degree in Computer Science at UCLA.

I have a broad interest in software engineering, spanning front-end development, back-end systems, and machine learning. I am especially enthusiastic about game development.

When it comes to art, I am drawn to animation, more specifically storyboarding. I believe that the story and messages of a creative work are some of its most important aspects, so I aim to highlight these elements in my own projects.

My aspiration is to one day create something meaningful that not only resonates with me but also has the ability to impact people's emotions and perspectives on the world.
