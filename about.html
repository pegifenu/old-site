About

Hello! I go by Pegifenu (Peg for short) on the Internet. I'm a programmer and artist currently pursuing a degree in Computer Science at UCLA.

I love problem solving and creating new things, so I'm interested in all aspects of software engineering, spanning front-end development, back-end systems, game development (especially!), you name it.

When it comes to art, I'm most drawn to animation, more specifically storyboarding. I believe that some of the most important aspects of a creative work are its story and messages.

My aspiration is to one day create something meaningful that not only resonates with me but also has the ability to impact people's emotions and perspectives on the world. In essence, I am always looking for the next big thing to pour my passion into.
